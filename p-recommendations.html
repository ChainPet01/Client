ChainPet Invitational Contest

To listen to the community, to expand the community, and to give back to our existing players, we are holding our first ChainPet Invitational Competition.During the event, ChainPet players will be rewarded with a substantial spending rebate by inviting other new players to join the game and make purchases!

Time: May 8 ~ June 5
Rule: invite new players to join during the event, and you can participate by spending.The activity lasts for one month, divided into weekly list, monthly total list.Rewards are ranked by the number of invitations players receive.

*All interested users can participate in the invitation-ranking contest
*New player: Wallet hasn't been logged into ChainPet before
*Valid Users: Purchase elves blind boxes to participate in the game during the campaign period

Weekly Ranking awards

NO. 1 30 percent of the amount of money valid invited
NO.2: 25 percent of the amount of money valid invited
NO.3: 15 percent of the amount of money valid invited
NO.4~NO.10: 10 percent of the amount of money valid invited
NO.10~NO.50: 8 percent of the amount of money valid invited
NO.50~NO.100: 5 percent of the amount of money valid invited

*for example:
1. Player A invites 10 new users in the first week, spends 10BNB effectively, ranks first in the first week, and gets 10BNB*30%=3BNB
2. Player A invites 10 new users in week 1 and 10 new users in week 2.These 20 users spend 20BNB effectively in week 2, and player A is rewarded 20BNB*25%=5BNB for ranking second

Monthly ranking rewards mystery gifts


In this competition, the top users in the monthly invitation will have a mysterious prize!Please check the event announcement for details.
In the invitational mechanics of the game itself, the first level invitational gets 5% and the second level invitational gets 2%.
This reward does not overlap with the event reward, so for the duration of the event, the first level invitation can receive up to 35% of the consumption rebate reward!
Lucky list: During the contest, any player who has a valid invitation will be counted as ChainPet lucky player and will receive a secret gift!
